Stage 4: Check what you have done

Control measures should be reviewed regularly to ensure that they remain effective. A joint management and safety representative committees can be an effective forum for this.

Violent incidents should be recorded and examined regularly to check whether progress is being made and whether the issue is under control.

If the active and reactive monitoring data indicates an ongoing problem return to the early stages of the process to ensure proper understanding of the issues and to identify additional preventive measures that could work.
