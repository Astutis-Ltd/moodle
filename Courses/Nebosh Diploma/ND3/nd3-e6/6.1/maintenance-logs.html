Maintenance logs

Maintenance logs are a practical way of demonstrating that work equipment is subject to regular maintenance. They are not legally required, but if used they must be kept up to date.

Some equipment may not be owned, but hired, by the user. Both the hire company and the user should establish between themselves who will carry out safety-related maintenance, and the terms of the agreement should be set out / recorded in writing with both parties should agree exactly what they are responsible for.
