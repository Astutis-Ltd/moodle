Organic management practice and structure

Specialisation

  • Low
  • No hard boundaries
  • Few different jobs.

Standardisation

  • Low
  • Individuals decide own methods.

Orientation of members

  • Goals.

Conflict resolution

  • Interaction.

Authority, control and communication

  • Wide net based upon common commitment.

Locus of superior competence

  • Wherever there is skill and competence.

Interaction

  • Lateral.

Communication content

  • Advice and information.

Loyalty

  • To project and group.

Prestige

  • From personal contribution.
