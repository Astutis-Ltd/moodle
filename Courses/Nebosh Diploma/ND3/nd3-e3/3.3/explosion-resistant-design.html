Explosion resistant design

Containers and connections can theoretically be designed to withstand the maximum explosion pressure that may be generated. However, because of cost considerations this is normally restricted to new and small pieces of plant.

BSEN 1127-1:20011 Explosive atmospheres – Explosion prevention and protection. Basic concepts and methodology defines two classes of equipment, protective systems and components:

  • Explosion-pressure-resistant equipment, which can withstand the expected explosion pressure without becoming permanently deformed
  • Explosion-pressure-shock-resistant equipment, which can withstand the expected explosion pressure, but may become permanently deformed.
Explosion relief

Explosion relief vents

A simple and common method of protecting process plant against the consequences of an internal dust explosion is to provide one or more deliberate points of weakness, called explosion relief vents.

If they are of suitable size and in the right place, they will safely vent an explosion within the plant. The intention is to prevent injuries to people nearby by avoiding uncontrolled failure of equipment.

Explosion panels

Explosion panels are designed to burst at a precisely defined bursting pressure. They have low inertia and provide full venting in around ten milliseconds. They are leak tight and have no mechanical parts, so are maintenance free.

Explosion relief from buildings – HSE’s traditional advice for buildings storing flammable liquids has been to ask for a lightweight roof, which will act as explosion relief if a vapour cloud ignited within the building.

Bursting discs

A bursting disc is also an ‘engineered weak spot’ within a pressurised system which can be made to burst at a pre-determined pressure and temperature.

It is a non-re-closing, one time device used to protect a system from overpressure damage or imploding, due to excessive positive or vacuum (negative) pressures in liquid or gaseous systems.

Bursting discs are a low cost solution with a range of benefits over other relief systems. They are leak tight, reliable and maintenance free, and provide an instantaneous response to over pressure.
