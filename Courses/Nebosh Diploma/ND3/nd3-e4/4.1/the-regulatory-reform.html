The Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005

The Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005 was made under the Regulatory Reform Act 2001. It replaced most fire safety legislation with one simple order, requiring any person who has some level of control of premises to take reasonable steps to reduce the risk from fire, and to ensure that people can safely escape if there is a fire.
