Part 1 – General

Article 3: Meaning of ‘responsible person’

Responsible person means:

  • the employer, if the workplace is to any extent under his control
  • the occupier or owner of the workplace, in so far as the workplace is under his control.

Article 4: Meaning of ‘general fire precautions’

General fire precautions means:

  • measures to reduce the risk of fire and the risk of fire spread on the premises
  • means of escape and measures for securing the safe and effective use of the means of escape
  • measures for fighting fires
  • measures for detecting fire and giving warning
  • measures relating to the instruction and training of employees
  • measures to mitigate the effects of fire.

Article 5: Duties under the order

The responsible person has the duty to ensure that the fire safety duties are complied with, so far as the matters are under his control.
