Article 17 - 22

Article 17: Maintenance

The premises and any facilities, equipment and devices provided must be subject to a suitable system of maintenance and be maintained in an efficient state, in efficient working order and in good repair.

Article 18: Safety assistance

The responsible person must appoint one or more competent persons to assist him in undertaking the preventive and protective measures.

Article 19: Provision of information to employees

    Employees must be provided with comprehensible and relevant information on:

  • the risks to them identified by the risk assessment
  • the preventive and protective measures
  • the procedures for serious and imminent danger
  • the identities of those persons providing competent safety assistance
  • the risks notified to him by other employers sharing the workplace.

Article 20: Provision of information to employers and the self-employed from outside undertakings

    The employer of any employees from an outside undertaking who are working in or on the premises must be provided with comprehensible and relevant information on:

  • the risks to those employees
  • the preventive and protective measures taken by the responsible person.

Article 21: Training

    Employees must be provided with adequate safety training:

  • when they are first employed
  • on their being exposed to new or increased risks because of:
    • job transfer or change of responsibilities
    • introduction of new work equipment or a change of existing work equipment
    • the introduction of new technology
    • the introduction of a new system of work or changing an existing system of work.

The training must:

  • include instruction and training on the appropriate precautions
  • be repeated periodically where appropriate.

Article 22: Co-operation and coordination

Where two or more responsible persons share, or have duties in respect of, premises (whether on a temporary or a permanent basis) each such person must co-operate with the other responsible person concerned, so far as is necessary, to enable them to comply with the requirements of the RRFSO.
