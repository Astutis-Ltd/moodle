Barriers

Where it is not practicable to use enclosing guards, barriers may be used to prevent people reaching the danger zone. These rely on a combination of height and distance to achieve their purpose. A height of 1.8m is recommended for perimeter fencing.

When reaching over a safety barrier, calculation of the safety distance requires three measurements:

  • the height of the danger zone (a)
  • the height of the protective structure (b)
  • the horizontal distance (c).
