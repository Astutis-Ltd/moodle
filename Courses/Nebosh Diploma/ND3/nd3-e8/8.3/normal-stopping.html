Normal stopping and emergency stop

Normal stopping

Each machine must be fitted with a control whereby the machine can be brought safely to a complete stop. The stop control must have priority over the start controls.

Emergency stop

Each machine must be fitted with one or more emergency stop devices to enable actual or impending danger to be averted, unless it would not lessen the risk. The emergency stop device must:

  • Have clearly identifiable, clearly visible and quickly accessible controls
  • Stop the dangerous process as quickly as possible, without creating additional hazards
  • Where necessary, trigger or permit the triggering of certain safeguard movements.

Once the emergency stop control has operated it must not be possible to reactivate the machine without properly disengaging the emergency stop device and properly restarting.
