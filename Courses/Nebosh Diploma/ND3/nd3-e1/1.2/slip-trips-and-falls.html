Slips, trips and falls on the same level

In 2019 / 20 slips and trips accounted for 29% of all RIDDOR reported non-fatal injuries (over 200 000 reports). Slips and trips are estimated to account for around one million lost work days per year in the UK.

HSE statistics suggest that most of these accidents are slips, most of which occur when floor surfaces are contaminated with water, talc, or grease, etc.

Slips occur when the foot and floor cannot make effective contact, usually when something has been spilled, or when the shoe sole and floor finish are incompatible.

Trips occur when an obstruction prevents normal movement of the foot resulting in loss of balance, usually caused by objects on the floor or uneven surfaces.
