Maintenance and test records

There is no explicit legal requirement to keep maintenance logs for portable and transportable electrical equipment.

A suitable log is a useful management tool for:

  • monitoring and reviewing the effectiveness of the maintenance scheme
  • demonstrating that a scheme exists
  • use as an inventory of equipment and a check on the use of unauthorised equipment.
