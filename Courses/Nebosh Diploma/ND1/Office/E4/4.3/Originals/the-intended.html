The intended benefits of ERM


For all types of organisations, there is a need to understand the risks being taken when seeking to achieve objectives and attain the desired level of reward.

The overall level of risk embedded within an organisation processes and activities needs to be fully understood. Significant risks and weaknesses in critical controls must be identified and prioritised for action to ensure the organisations survival in the first instance and thereafter its sustained success.

Effectiveness is about the achievement of planned outcomes, goals, or objectives – Effective organisations achieve their goals.

Efficiency is about the ratio of input to output, an efficient organisation gets more from its efforts, and generates less waste.

Efficacy indicates the power or capacity to achieve a desired outcome (i.e. to be effective).

The expected benefits of any risk management initiative should be determined in advance.

In general terms the outputs from successful risk management include compliance, assurance and enhanced decision-making. These outputs will provide benefits by way of:

‣
effectiveness of tactics

‣
improvements in the efficiency of operations

‣
efficacy of the strategy of the organisation.
