Glossary of terms


The key terms, as used in ISO 31000 â€“ Risk management (adapted from ISO Guide 73) and BS 31100 Risk management: Code of practice and guidance for the implementation of BS ISO 31000 are presented in the below table.

Risk mitigation Measures taken to reduce an undesired consequence.
Risk avoidance Decision not to be involved in, or withdraw from an activity based on the level of risk.
Risk retention Acceptance of the benefit of gain or burden of loss from a particular risk. (Includes the acceptance of residual risk)
Risk transfer Sharing with another party the burden of loss or benefit of gain for a risk. (Might be achieved through legislation, contract, insurance or other means)
Risk sharing Form of risk treatment involving the agreed distribution of risks with other parties.
Risk financing A form of risk treatment involving contingent arrangements for the provision of funds to meet the financial consequences should they occur.
