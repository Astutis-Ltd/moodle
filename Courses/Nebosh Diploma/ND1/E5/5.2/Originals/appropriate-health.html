Appropriate health and safety objectives


An organisations goals for improving health and safety performance need to be implemented by a series of linked plans and objectives that cascade down through the various levels within the organisation.

A prerequisite of effective health and safety plans and objectives is that they should be SMART:

‣
specific

‣
measurable

‣
attainable

‣
realistic/relevant

‣
timebound.


What are likely to be appropriate and effective objectives for a given organisation will depend on its hazard burden and risk profile and the size of the gap between its current arrangements for managing health and safety and good practise.
