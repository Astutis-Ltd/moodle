Annualisation

Annualisation is the process of determining the average cost and the average benefit per year. It is done by summing up all discounted costs and all discounted benefits over the appraisal period and by dividing the outcomes by the length of the appraisal period.

In general, the annual cost will be smaller than the actual cost in the first year and larger than the actual cost in subsequent years. The annual benefit will generally be of similar magnitude of the actual benefit accruing each year.
