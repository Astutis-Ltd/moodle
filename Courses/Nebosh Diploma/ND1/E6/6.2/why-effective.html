Why effective communication is important

The art of effective communication is getting the right message (in the right format), to the right audience at the right time.

Effective communication is vital in health and safety management to ensure that workers are fully informed of workplace hazards and risks, risk control measures and expected behaviours to keep themselves and others safe. It is equally important to ensure that lessons learned from incidents are shared locally and elsewhere in the organisation to prevent similar future incidents causing more harm.
