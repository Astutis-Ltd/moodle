Engaging with stakeholders

Health and safety professionals should play a leading role in stakeholder engagement, including collaborative working to build business benefit at whatever level they operate. Developing networks inside and outside of the business is essential in building and maintaining positive relationships to add strategic benefit to the business.

As discussed earlier ISO45001 requires organisations to understand the external and internal issues that may affect the organisations operations, including the needs and expectations of workers and other stakeholders.

In this context, health and safety professionals work collaboratively with stakeholders to build a robust organisational structure, assign responsibilities, plan activities, develop safe systems of work and implement management procedures.
