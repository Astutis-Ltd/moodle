Organisational context and health and
safety responsibilities


ISO45001 requires organisations to determine the external and internal issues that are relevant to its purpose and that may affect its ability to achieve the intended outcome(s) of the management system. This includes determining the relevant needs and expectations of workers and any other stakeholders.

This context is typically determined through a SWOT analysis to understand opportunities and threats in the operating environment and current strengths and weaknesses within the organisation. A PESTLE analysis provides further information on external variables that may impact upon the organisation and the way it works.

A high-level understanding of the key internal and external issues that can positively or negatively affect health and safety within the organisation and how they interact is essential before planning and implementing controls within the system.
