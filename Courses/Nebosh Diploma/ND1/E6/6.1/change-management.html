Change management

Where organisational changes affect the way work is done, throughout the change and/or after the change the risk assessments for the affected work will require review and possible revision. The health and safety professional(s) should be consulted early on the possible impacts the changes will have on health and safety and be involved in the change management project to ensure that health and safety risks are properly managed throughout.

The Plan, Do, Check, Act management control cycle offers a useful structure for planning and implementing a change and for reviewing the effectiveness of the change and driving any necessary further actions.
