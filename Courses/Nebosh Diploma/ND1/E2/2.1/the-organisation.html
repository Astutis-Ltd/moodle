The organisation as a system

A system is a group of interacting, interrelated, or interdependent elements arranged in an orderly manner to form a coherent whole. Systems may involve the movement of matter, energy, or information through defined pathways.

Systems theory relates to the study of self-regulating systems (i.e. those that self-correct their behaviour through feedback). Self-regulating systems are found in many areas of nature and science, for example, biology, sociology, meteorology, engineering and IT, as well as organisational theory.
