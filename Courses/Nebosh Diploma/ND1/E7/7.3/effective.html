Effective management of organisational change

There are many drivers for organisational change including:

  • responding to opportunities and threats in the operating environment
  • economic recession or depression
  • shareholder requirements
  • changes in legislation or other standards
  • technological developments.

Organisational changes such as introducing new technology, reducing staffing levels, using contractors or outsourcing, combining departments, or changes to roles and responsibilities can, if inadequately conceived or implemented, have a detrimental effect on health and safety during the change process and into the new future state.
