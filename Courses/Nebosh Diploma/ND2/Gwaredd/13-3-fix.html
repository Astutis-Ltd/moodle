Legal requirements (Part 1)

The Ionising Radiations Regulations 2017 implement the Basic Safety Standards Directive 2013/59/Euratom in Great Britain.

Ionising Radiation Regulations 2017 (IRR)

The Ionising Radiations Regulations 2017 establish a framework for ensuring that exposure to ionising radiation arising from all work activities is kept as low as reasonably practicable, and does not exceed dose limits specified for individuals.

The Regulations are extensive, consisting of 42 Regulations, presented in seven parts, plus eight schedules and two appendices as detailed the table below.

Some of the key requirements are outlined thereafter.
