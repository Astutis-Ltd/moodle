Radiation Protection Adviser (RPA) (Regulation 14)

Where work with ionising radiation is notifiable to the HSE, a suitably certificated and experienced Radiation Protection Advisor (RPA) must be appointed.

Radiation employers need to consult a suitable RPA for advice on complying with the requirements of the regulations. Matters on which advice from a suitable RPA should be sought include:

  • risk assessments
  • designation of controlled and supervised areas
  • incident investigations
  • contingency planning
  • dose assessment and recording.
