<p><strong>Booking your exams</strong>
</p>
<p>If you haven’t already paid for and booked your NEBOSH exams, then we recommend that you do so as soon as you are ready. You have a full 12-months access to your course from the date of purchase. You must complete the course, register, and sit the exam within the 12-months
</p>
<p>You must register to sit the exams by completing the booking form eight- weeks before the exam date. Booking your exams couldn’t be easier.
