  Starting your course

Start the course by working your way through the Overview section, this details how the course works, how you can find your way around and where to go for help. It also includes important details about the assessment.

You have 6 months from today to complete the course, including the assessment. If you do not complete the course in this time and require an extension, you will need to contact us for guidance.

  Booking your exams

You will need to book your exam submission date before the booking deadline for that date. Visit our exam hub for details of submission dates, booking deadlines and to complete the exam booking form.

Once you have completed the assessment it will be sent to NEBOSH to be verified. It is not until we have received verification of your marks from NEBOSH that we can confirm whether you have passed, or not.
